Planning focus

This dashboard is meant to reduce cognitive overload by bringing together planning, progress, and resource discovery in one workspace.

  • Goals define what you want to achieve this semester.
  • Projects break each goal into manageable efforts.
  • Milestones show the next concrete checkpoints.
  • Saved resources keep helpful links attached to your work.
